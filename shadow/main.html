Founded by Priyanka Vasudev, Fran Aguila, and Sheen Sagalongos, Kanekta is a consciously curated B2B marketplace that connects ethical fashion brands operating out of sweatshop prone regions to retailers across North America.

Our mission is to normalize ethical purchasing within the mainstream market by empowering both local artisans and their surrounding communities. Kanekta's ultimate goal is to foster producer growth, boost consumer consciousness and shift retail buying practices to support ethical alternatives.
