Kanekta is an agency representing artisan brands from across the globe within the Canadian apparel industry. Our mission is to increase the availability of ethically-sourced products in the mainstream market by connecting and managing the relationship between producers and retailers. Kanekta's ultimate goal is to foster producer growth, boost consumer consciousness and shift retail buying practices to support ethical alternatives.
